our story...

June 13th, 2016. The start of summer. The start of... GIRLS WHO CODE!

Ever since the beginning of this program... Kaitlyn, Ashi, Lili and Nichole have had a burning passion for making the world better.

They created the idea Find the Light, a website that promotes global connection and social awareness.

With multiple resources, statistics, and volunteering events that are updated with the most recent news and ideas, Find the Light is not only a way to discover the world around you... but a way to discover your own interests.

our vision...

We hope this website allows people to...

  • expand their interests and discover new ideas.

  • form new friendships, and meet new people.

  • to expand their knowledge and passions.

And finally, we hope this website allows people to find their own light.
